# LiteAcad 🎓
> Revolutionizing Academic Systems through Digital Innovation.

LiteAcad is a cutting-edge digital ecosystem engineered to modernize academic administration and elevate the learning experience within the Nigerian educational sector. We bridge the gap between traditional classroom settings and the digital future by providing robust, intuitive, and highly scalable software solutions for secondary schools and tertiary institutions.

---

## 🚀 The Vision
Our mission is to empower educators to focus on teaching while our technology handles the complexity of modern school management. LiteAcad centralizes administrative tasks and democratizes access to high-quality learning resources.

## 📦 Product Suite

### 1. Integrated School Portal
The digital backbone of your institution. 
* **Modern Web Presence:** Fully responsive school website to boost visibility.
* **Result Management:** Automated computation and grading system.
* **Records:** Seamless management of student and staff data.
* **Finance:** Integrated fee tracking and ledger system.
* **Admin Dashboard:** Centralized control for easy updates.

### 2. LiteAcad CBT (Computer Based Test)
A professional simulation platform designed for the Nigerian exam environment.
* **Exam Fidelity:** Mirrors the exact look and feel of JAMB, WAEC, GCE, and Post-UTME.
* **Preparation:** Helps students master exam pressure and timing.
* **Admin Tools:** Effortless subject management and question bank curation.

### 3. LiteAcad Digital Library
A 24/7 research hub accessible from any device.
* **Rich Content:** Textbooks, lecture handouts, past projects, and academic journals.
* **Productivity:** In-app annotations, bookmarks, and reading progress tracking.
* **Offline Access:** Download materials to study without active data usage.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Stack

LiteAcad is built using modern, high-performance technologies to ensure reliability and scalability:

* **Frontend:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
* **Animations:** [AOS (Animate On Scroll)](https://michalsnik.github.io/aos/)
* **Backend:** [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/) & [Express.js](https://expressjs.com/)
* **Database:** [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/)
* **Architecture:** MERN Stack (High availability and data integrity)
